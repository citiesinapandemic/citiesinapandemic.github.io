Overview of our Project

We are a group of Austrian students that were originally planning to travel to Kuala Lumpur/Malaysia and bangkok/Thailand to get to know city life better and to do research on urban development and planning.
Unfortunately, due to Covid-19 and the imposed travel restrictions, the trip has been cancelled. Nevertheless, we are curious about your city and everyday life in your urban environemnt. Furthermore we ware interested in how Covid-19 affects you and your city. Therefore, we would like to invite you to take part in our "remote" research project! Although we are not on site to get in touch with you personally, we prepared different digital tools you can easyl use for sharing your expperiences with us. We have different activities planned so you can choose on of the following options of participation and let us know what is on your mind and what you think about urban futures.

Note: the required language is English.
