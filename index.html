Welcome to Cities in a Pandemic

A research project regarding to daily life of local students with the corona pandemic in the city of Bangkok and Kuala Lumpur

We need your help!

We are six Austrian students from the University of Innsbruck who are interested in the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on students in Kuala Lumpur (KL) and Bangkok (BK). Our study is part of a research project at the University of Innsbruck in Austria that deals with urban futures in Southeast Asia. There are three main objectives that we follow with our research. First, we want to get a deeper insight how the COVID-19 pandemic and the restrictions of the government affect the daily life of students in KL and BK in general. Our second aim is to find out if and how the students’ perception and interaction with their environment has changed since the start of the pandemic. Finally, we raise the question if the crisis has led to a reassessment of personal needs regarding the city and urban space. In other words, how should future urban planning look like to deal with crisis, like a pandemic?
