Welcome to Urban-Planning COV Group

A research project regarding to daily life of local students with the corona pandemic in the city of Bangkok and Kuala Lumpur

We need your help!

We are a student research team form the University in Innsbruck (Austria) and we are working on the topic: disruptions in times of COVID-19 in your daily life.
The research design is based on digital tools such as video conference programs. If you are interested see below the different possibilities to participat If you want to participate pls contact us at MAILADRESS
